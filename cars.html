Wheels

Electronics

  • LED display.
  • RD0901 ECU

Tyres

  • 20.5 x 7 x 13 racing slicks.

Chassis

  • Tubular space frame.
  • AISI4130 steel pipes.

Engine

  • KTM Duke390.

Power

  • 32kW @ 9500 rpm

Torque

  • 35Nm @ 7250 rpm

Body

  • Aluminium Sheet

Ergonomics

  • Driver size adjustments-Fixed seat and steering wheel. Pedal adjust fore and aft 30 mm from central position.
  • Driver visibility-210 degree side visibility.

Suspension & Steering

  • Suspension type- Double A-arm pull rod actuated horizontally oriented spring and damper at the rear and front.
  • Wheels- 13 inch, 3 pc Al Rim-Mq center, 4.25 inch.
  • Aluminium steering wheel with quick release.
  • Motion ratio-Progressive type.
  • Steering rack ratio of 6.33:1

Transmission

  • Drexler Limited Slip Clutch type Differential with 6 torque biasing ratio.
  • Hollow heat-treated steel half shafts
  • 6-Speed sequential gearbox.
  • Final Drive Ratio of 3.69

Brakes

  • Rotors (Front)-Disc Brake System; PS1 Wilwood Calipers
  • Rotors (Rear)-Disc Brake System; PS1 Wilwood Calipers
  • Upright Assembly-CNC Al-7075 T6, Integral caliper and wheel speed sensor mount
